KEY POINT 01

CRPS compares the full predictive distribution with the observed outcome — not just the mean.

KEY POINT 02

Lower CRPS means better probabilistic prediction — rewarding both accuracy and calibrated uncertainty.

KEY POINT 03

For deterministic point predictions, CRPS reduces exactly to MAE. It generalises the absolute error to distributions.
